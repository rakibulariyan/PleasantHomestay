Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2023

1. Information We Collect

At Pleasant HomeStay, we collect information to provide better services to all our guests. The types of personal information we collect include:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment information and billing address
  • Government-issued identification for verification
  • Preferences and special requests

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Device information and operating system

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process your bookings and manage your stay
  • To communicate with you about your reservation
  • To improve our services and guest experience
  • To send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our guests and property
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure socket layer (SSL) technology
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure data storage systems

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time
