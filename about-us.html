Our Story

Founded in 2018, Pleasant HomeStay began as a simple vision: to create a home away from home for travelers visiting Guwahati. What started as a small family-run guesthouse has grown into one of the most trusted and beloved accommodation options in Hatigaon.

Our journey began when our founders, Mr. and Mrs. Sharma, recognized the need for comfortable, affordable, and personalized accommodation in Guwahati. They transformed their family property into a welcoming space that combines the comfort of home with the hospitality of a premium stay.

Today, we continue to uphold the same values that guided us from the beginning - genuine care for our guests, attention to detail, and a commitment to creating memorable experiences.
