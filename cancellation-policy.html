Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 2023

Standard Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our guests and our business.

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Conditions
More than 7 days before check-in 100% refund Full refund of advance payment
48 hours to 7 days before check-in 50% refund Half of the advance payment refunded
24 to 48 hours before check-in 25% refund Quarter of advance payment refunded
Less than 24 hours or no-show No refund Full advance payment retained

Special Circumstances

Peak Season & Holidays

During peak season (December-January, major festivals), the following policies apply:

  • Free cancellation up to 14 days before check-in
  • 50% refund for cancellations 7-14 days before check-in
  • No refund for cancellations within 7 days of check-in

Long Stays (7+ nights)

  • Free cancellation up to 30 days before check-in
  • 50% refund for cancellations 15-30 days before check-in
  • 25% refund for cancellations 7-14 days before check-in
  • No refund for cancellations within 7 days

Modification Policy

We try to be flexible with changes to your reservation:

  • Date changes subject to availability
  • No fee for modifications made more than 48 hours in advance
  • Modifications within 48 hours treated as cancellation
  • Rate differences apply for room upgrades

Force Majeure

In case of unforeseen circumstances beyond our control:

  • Natural disasters or extreme weather conditions
  • Government travel restrictions
  • Unexpected property closures

In such cases, we offer:

  • Full refund or credit for future stay
  • Flexible rescheduling options
  • Case-by-case consideration

How to Cancel

To cancel your reservation:

  • Call us at +91 98765 43210
  • Email us at info@pleasanthomestay.com
  • Provide your booking reference number
  • Allow 5-7 business days for refund processing
